Pempek

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ground mackarel
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • tapioca
  • 1 kg palm sugar, diced
  • bird's eye chili
  • 200 g garlic
  • 3 tbsp white vinegar
  • salt
  • 2 liters of water

Steps

  1. Mix ground mackarel with water and salt
  2. Add tapioca until the dough can be kneaded. The more tapioca, the more chewy it will be
  3. Shape the dough to the taste
  4. Simmer in a medium heat until pempek floats
  5. Put the pempek into the cold water
  6. Simmer together palm sugar and some water until the palm sugar melts and mixes with the water
  7. Ground the onion and the chili. Add them to the sugar
  8. Add vinegar and the salt
  9. Poach in a low heat for an hour until it thickens. Filter and set aside
  10. Serve pempek with cuko sauce
